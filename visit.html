From beginnings in Beijing, China, we're now excited to be serving coffee
and brunch in Brisbane on the Australian East Coast.

From flagship roastery-cafes, to CBD espresso bars, each
Industry Beans venue is unique to its location and surroundings.
Our coffee menu rotates fortnightly across each of our cafes,
and our brunch menu relaunches every three months to
reflect the changing seasons around us. We invite you to visit.
